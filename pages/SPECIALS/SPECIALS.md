**1\) Technical Property Survey Before Purchase**  
Before purchasing a property, I provide a comprehensive technical survey, which I personally coordinate with trusted professionals. We thoroughly examine the structural condition, mechanical systems, moisture issues, and any potential renovation risks. My aim is to ensure that my client has a clear understanding of possible future costs and hidden risks. A detailed and easy-to-understand report is provided following the inspection. This allows you to make a safe and well-informed decision before buying.

**2\) Identification of Hidden Defects**  
Beyond visible issues, I place strong emphasis on identifying hidden defects. Drawing on my experience and working with specialist partners, we investigate structural cracks, damp problems, insulation deficiencies, and previously poorly executed repairs. These issues are often not visible at first glance but can lead to significant expenses later. My goal is to prevent unpleasant surprises after completion. All findings are properly documented and clearly explained to the client.

**3\) Waterproofing Solutions**  
Due to the climate in the Costa Blanca region, damp and water ingress can be common problems. Together with my own team, I provide professional waterproofing solutions for roofs, terraces, basements, and walls. We use modern materials and proven technologies to ensure long-term protection. I personally supervise each project to guarantee high standards of workmanship. Our objective is to preserve and enhance the value of your property.

**4\) Court Expert Support**  
If a seller has concealed relevant defects or information during the sale, we provide court expert support. Together with my certified expert colleague, we prepare an official technical report regarding the identified issues. All necessary technical evidence is professionally documented and suitable for use in legal proceedings. We firmly represent our clients’ interests in dispute situations. Our aim is to ensure that buyers receive full professional and legal protection.
